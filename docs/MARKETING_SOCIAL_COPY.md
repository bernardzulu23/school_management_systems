## Tips for posting

1. **Post timing:** Early term or 6–8 weeks before ECZ SBA submission — urgency feels real.
2. **WhatsApp:** Send in teacher/admin groups; ask one respected member to endorse it first.
3. **Facebook:** Add 1 screenshot (SBA dashboard or deadline tracker) — posts with visuals get more clicks.
4. **Be accurate:** Always say **2-month free trial** (not 30 days — billing uses 60 days).
5. **Solo teachers:** They register learners themselves with one-time codes — there is no public student self-signup.
